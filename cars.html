Lmp1:

A Le Mans Prototype (LMP) is a type of sports prototype race car used in various races and championships, including the 24 Hours of Le Mans, FIA World Endurance Championship, IMSA SportsCar Championship, European Le Mans Series and Asian Le Mans Series. Le Mans Prototypes were created by the Automobile Club de l'Ouest (ACO). The technical requirements for an LMP include bodywork covering all mechanical elements of the car. The lmp1 cars were the fastest of the le mans prototypes. The lmp1 class in is normally seen as the most prestigious class in wich the top manufacturers like Porsche, Audi and Bentley compete agianst one another for the victory in their class but also the overall vicory. in lmp1 the manufacturesr can design their chassis themselves as long as it is complaint with some safety requirements.
